About Me

Hello! I am a senior currently studying computer science at the University of Michigan. Throughout my time at Michigan, I have had the opportunity to work in multidisiplinary teams to develop video games. Currently, I am the leader of the Player team for WolverineSoft Studio's upcoming project Desolation Place; our first 3D horror game. My responsibilities include assigning tasks amongst the 5 members of my team to develop core features of the player's experience. Moreover, I am responsible for mentoring my teammates, and ensuring they are supported throughout the development process.

Experience

I have experience doing game programming in C# and Unity; and have developed 3 projects using it. I have developed code for gameplay, artificial intelligence, audio, and user interfaces. I also have extensive experience programming in C++, Python, and Javascript. My coursework has been focused on game development, operating systems and computer security.
