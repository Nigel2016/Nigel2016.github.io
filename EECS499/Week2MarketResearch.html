Market Research

This week, I did research on Market sectors and Relevant companies which my research may fall in. This information can be useful for identifying potential audiences and stakeholders that would be interested in my work.

Relevant Market Sectors

For this research project, I am investigating how tools powered by procedural generation can be used to automate level design. Given this, it seems that the following markets may be relevant:

  • Video Game Industry
  • AI (artificiant intelligence is usually necessary for procedural generation)
As of 2019, the game development industry was "valued at USD 151.55 billion in 2019 and is expected to reach a value of USD 256.97 billion by 2025."(Mordor Intelligence) The industry is composed of thousands of companies, both in the AAA development (Microsoft, Playstation, Nintendo) and independent, small scale, development (Heart Shaped Games, Studio MDHR). The AI industry is smaller in market share, but covers a wider range of business applications (Health Care, Robotics, Security). The global market share of AI is on track to reach USD 169,441.8 Million in 2025 (Valuates Reports)

Relevant Products

I also took a look into some procedural generation products that exist within the Game Industry market in particular. Some tools are made with AAA studios and large scale level design in mind. Others are free tools which smaller development teams can take adantage of:

Ghost of Tsushima

An engineer at Sucker Punch Games who worked on Ghost of Tsushima was interviewed by WolverineSoft over the summer. One of his responsibilies included working on proedural generation tools to help automate the creation of the open world. It makes sense that these sorts of tools would be necessary; the world of Ghost of Tsushima is so large that it would take a significant amount of time to build by hand.

World Creator

World Creator is a Terrain and Landscape generator that can be used to automatically create natural environments in real time.

3D scene Procedural Engine

A general purpose tool that can be used to procedurally generate a wide variety of designs (roads, buildings, furniture assets). It useds a Node-based language that is visual; unlike text-based scripting, control and logic can be built using node structures and graphs. This allows for a designer to more intuitively develop procedural assests without being bogged down by semantics of languages like C++ or C#.

World Maker

World Maker Is a 2D tool that can be used to automatically generate 2D maps. Once the user specifies some options (map size, number of contienents), the tool will quickly build a map poplated with islands, randomized climate, country identifiers (flags), etc. The tool also permits users to modify the maps contents once it has been generated.

One Page Dungeon

A free Procedural Generation Tool that can automatically generate 2D dungeons. The tool provdes a simple interface and control scheme to modify the resulting maps contents, such as adding/increasing water levels and adding secret rooms. It also provide designers with 2 options for exporting the map to their games (png or JSON).
