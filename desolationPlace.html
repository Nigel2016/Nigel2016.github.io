About Desolation Place

Desolation Place is a 3D first-person stealth horror game where the player character must traverse through an abandoned castle, avoiding both the horrors that lurk within its shadows as well as the terrors that lurk within his past.

Development Details

  • Studio: WolverineSoft
  • Development Cycle: ~10 weeks w/ Agile Development
  • Team: 25 Developers as a whole; 5 on the Player Team
  • Engine: Unity
  • Programming Language: C#

Role: Studio Technical lead

  • Oversaw a sub-team of 5 developers in designing and implementing features for the player experience in Unity Engine and C#.
  • Assigned weekly tasks amongst the player team to develop essential gameplay mechanics, user interface, and audio via Jira.
  • Maintained communication with the level design and enemy teams daily to ensure player mechanics interacted well with other gameplay systems.
