About IO

IO is an 2D Action/Adventure game with challenging combat and weapon teleportation as a central mechanic. You step into the shoes of a young girl venturing into an ancient abyss, and discover the secrets of a lost civilization.

Development Details

  • Studio: WolverineSoft
  • Development Cycle: 3 months w/ Agile Development
  • Team: 57 Developers as a whole; 7 on the Enemies Team
  • Engine: Unity
  • Programming Language: C#

Role: AI Programmer

  • Programmed the attack and movement behaviors for two enemies present in the final game.
  • Fined Tuned the functionality of enemies in the game; determining their right level of difficulty and improving their design.
  • Revised the implementation of the ememy AI across an Agile development Cycle, iteratively improving their behavior.
